Bhuj & Rann of Kutch Schedule

Day-by-day active exploration details

Sample Itinerary

Arrive at Bhuj Airport/Station, where our tour representative welcomes you. Drive to your hotel, freshen up, and begin exploring the stunning Prag Mahal (Italian Gothic architecture) and Aina Mahal (mirror palace). Conclude with a gorgeous sunset at Bhujia Fort overlooking the city.

10:00 AM - Airport Pick-Up

Pick-up in private luxury AC SUV with safe guide.

05:30 PM - Bhujia Fort Walk

Panoramic sunset view of Kutch landscapes.

Drive towards the breathtaking White Rann of Kutch. En route, stop at Bhirandiyara village to taste authentic mawa sweets and explore traditional embroidery crafts in Hodka. Check in to your luxury tent at Rann. In the evening, witness the white salt flats glow under the sun.

11:00 AM - Handcraft Village

Interact with local Kutch artisans in Bhunga mud huts.

07:30 PM - Cultural Rann Program

Live folk music, traditional dances under moonlight.

Visit Kalo Dungar (Black Hill), the highest point in Kutch, which offers breathtaking panoramic views of the Indo-Pak border and the Great salt basin. Stop by the historic handicraft village of Gandhi Nu Dham before returning to Bhuj.

08:00 AM - Kalo Dungar Temple

Visit 400-year-old Lord Dattatreya shrine at the peak.

04:00 PM - Handicraft Shopping

Shop premium Ajrakh block prints and Rogan art directly.
